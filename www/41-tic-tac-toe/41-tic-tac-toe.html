How to Play
  • Players take turns placing X or O on the 3×3 grid.
  • First to get 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins!
  • If all 9 squares fill with no winner, it's a draw.
  • Easy: AI plays randomly. Medium: AI sometimes blunders. Hard: AI is unbeatable — best you can do is draw!
  • Switch Mode, Difficulty, or your Symbol anytime — board resets automatically.
  • Tap 👤 to view your profile, lifetime stats and achievements.
