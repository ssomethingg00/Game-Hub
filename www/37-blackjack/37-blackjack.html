❓ How to Play

Goal

Beat the dealer by getting closer to 21 without going over (busting).

Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10) = face value
  • J, Q, K = 10 points
  • Ace = 1 or 11 (whichever helps more)

Actions

  • Hit — Draw another card
  • Stand — Keep your hand, end your turn
  • Double Down — Double your bet, draw exactly one card
  • Split — Split a matching pair into 2 hands (equal bet per hand)
  • Insurance — When dealer shows Ace: side bet pays 2:1 if dealer has Blackjack

Payouts

Dealer Rules

Dealer must hit on 16 or less, and stands on 17 or more. One card is dealt face-down (hole card) and revealed after you finish.

Split Rules

  • Split any matching pair; equal second bet required
  • Up to 4 hands total via re-splitting
  • Split Aces get one card each only; 10 on split Ace = 21, not Blackjack
