How to Play — Reflex Rush

  1. Classic Reaction: A red screen appears. Wait patiently.
  2. When it turns green, tap/click anywhere (or press Space) as fast as possible.
  3. Tapping during the red phase = false start. Wait for green!
  4. After 5 trials you'll see your average ms and a rating from Slow to Superhuman.
  5. Tap Target: Circles appear — hit them before the blue bar empties.
  6. Catch Color: The banner shows a color. Find and tap that exact color in the grid as fast as you can. Wrong taps add a 200 ms penalty.
  7. Your best times are stored on this device — try to beat them!
