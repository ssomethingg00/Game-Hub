HOW TO PLAY

Break all the bricks to advance levels!

Controls

Power-ups

⬛ Wide — wider paddle (15s)

⬛ Multiball — 2 extra balls

⬛ Slow — slows ball (10s)

⬛ Laser — auto lasers (8s)

⬛ Fireball — breaks 2HP bricks (10s)

Scoring
