HOW TO PLAY

Stack falling blocks to complete horizontal lines. Complete lines to score points and advance levels!

← →Move piece left / right
↑ / XRotate clockwise
ZRotate counter-clockwise
↓Soft drop (score +1/row)
SpaceHard drop — instant placement
C / ShiftHold current piece for later
P / EscPause game

Ghost piece shows where your block will land. Use Hold (C) to save a piece for the right moment. Clearing 4 lines at once is a TETRIS — maximum points!

On mobile: tap canvas to rotate, swipe left/right to move, swipe down fast for hard drop.
