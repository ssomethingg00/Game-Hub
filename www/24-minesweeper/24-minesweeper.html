❓ How to Play

🔎
Reveal cells

Left-click (or tap) a covered cell to reveal it. Numbers show how many mines are adjacent. The first click is always safe!

🚩
Flag mines

Right-click (or long-press 400ms on mobile) to flag a cell you think is a mine. Use the Flag Mode button on mobile too.

⚡
Chord (speed reveal)

Click a revealed number when you've flagged exactly that many neighbors — it reveals all remaining adjacent cells at once.

🏆
Win condition

Reveal every non-mine cell to win. You don't need to flag all mines — just uncover the safe ones!

⎕
Keyboard (desktop)

Arrow keys to move focus, Enter/Space to reveal, F to flag, C to chord a number cell.
