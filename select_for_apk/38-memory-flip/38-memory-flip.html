Memory Flip ♠♥♣♦

How to Play

  • Click any face-down card to flip it.
  • Then click a second card — if they share the same rank and color (e.g. 7♥ + 7♦, or K♠ + K♣), they stay face-up.
  • If they don't match, they flip back — remember where they are!
  • Clear the whole board as quickly as possible with as few moves as possible.
  • Beat your personal best score on each difficulty.
  • Keyboard: Tab to navigate cards, Enter/Space to flip, Arrow keys to move.
