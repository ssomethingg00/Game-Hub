How to Play

The Board

8×8 grid. Black and White each start with 2 discs in the center.

Legal Move

You can only place a disc where it flanks one or more straight lines of opponent discs — with one of your discs at the other end.

Flipping

All flanked opponent discs are immediately flipped to your color.

Passing

If you have no legal move, your turn is automatically passed. If neither player can move, the game ends.

Winning

When the game ends, the player with the most discs wins.

Strategy Tips

  • Corners are gold — they can never be flipped!
  • Avoid the squares adjacent to empty corners (they give the opponent corners).
  • More legal moves (mobility) beats more discs early on.
