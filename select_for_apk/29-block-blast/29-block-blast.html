How to Play
🖱️ Drag a piece from the tray onto the board where it fits.
🔄 Tap a piece (without dragging) to rotate it 90°.
📐 Ghost preview shows green = valid, red = invalid.
💥 Fill any complete row or column — it clears and scores points!
⚡ Combo! Clear multiple lines at once for big bonus points.
🎯 Game Over when no piece in your tray fits anywhere.
▮▮ Pause anytime with the pause button in the HUD.
