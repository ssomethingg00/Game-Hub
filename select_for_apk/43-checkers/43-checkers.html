How to Play Checkers

Setup

  • Red pieces start at the bottom, Black at the top.
  • Pieces only move on dark squares.

Moving

  • Click a piece to select it — legal moves are highlighted.
  • Men (round pieces) move one square diagonally forward only.
  • Kings (♛) move diagonally in any direction.

Capturing (Mandatory!)

  • If you can jump over an enemy piece, you must.
  • Jump over an adjacent enemy to the empty square beyond — the enemy is removed.
  • If you can keep jumping with the same piece, you must continue.
  • Only jump squares are shown when captures are available.

Kinging

  • Reach the opponent's back row and your man becomes a King ♛.
  • Your turn ends when you're crowned, even mid-jump sequence.

Winning

  • Win when your opponent has no pieces left, or has no legal moves.
