📖 How to Play

1
A scrambled word appears as letter tiles. Click/tap tiles to move them into the answer slots below.
2
Tap a placed tile to remove it. Press Enter or hit Check Answer to submit.
3
Use Shuffle to rearrange tiles, Hint to reveal a letter (costs points), or Skip to move on (breaks streak).
4
Score = word length × 10 + speed bonus. Build a streak for up to ×3 multiplier!
5
Keyboard: type letters, Backspace removes, Enter submits, Space shuffles, H hints, Esc skips.
