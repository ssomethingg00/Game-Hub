How to Play Klondike Solitaire

Goal

Move all 52 cards to the 4 foundation piles (♠♥♦♣), each built A→2→...→K in suit.

Tableau

Build down in alternating colors (red on black, black on red). Only a King can start an empty column. Move any valid face-up sequence as a group.

Stock & Waste

Tap the stock to draw cards (Draw 1 or Draw 3 mode). Only the top waste card is playable. Tap the empty stock to recycle the waste.

Moving Cards

Drag & drop cards or sequences. Tap a card to select (gold glow), then tap a destination. Double-tap any card to auto-send it to the foundation if legal.

Scoring

Waste→Foundation: +10 | Tableau→Foundation: +10 | Flip card: +5 | Foundation→Tableau: −15

Controls

Undo — revert last action | Hint — show a valid move | D1/D3 — toggle draw mode | ⏸ — pause | ⚙ — settings | 👤 — profile
