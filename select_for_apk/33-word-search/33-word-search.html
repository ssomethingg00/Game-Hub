How to Play

  1. Look for words from the list hidden in the letter grid.
  2. Click and drag (or touch and slide) from the first letter to the last letter of a word.
  3. Words may run left, right, up, down, or diagonally. On Hard mode they can also run backwards.
  4. The word lights up and gets crossed off the list when found correctly.
  5. Stuck? Tap a word in the "Words to Find" list to briefly reveal where it is on the grid.
  6. Change the highlight colour any time from Settings ⚙.
  7. Find all words as fast as you can for a personal best time!
  8. Press P or use the pause button to pause the timer.
