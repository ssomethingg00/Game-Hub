How to Play
  • A secret word is hidden as blank dashes
  • Tap or type letters A–Z to guess
  • Correct letters fill in the blanks
  • Each wrong guess adds a part to the hangman figure
  • Running out of guesses = game over (reveal word, streak resets)
  • Tap the difficulty badge up top to switch Easy / Normal / Hard
  • Easy = 10 guesses + free hints · Normal = 8 · Hard = 6 (×1.6 score)
  • Guess the full word to win and earn points!
  • Use 💡 Hint to reveal a letter (cost depends on difficulty)
  • Build a streak for score multipliers!
