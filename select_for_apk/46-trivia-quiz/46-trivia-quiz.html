How to Play 📖

  • Pick a category and difficulty, then tap Start.
  • Answer 10 questions — pick the correct option before the timer runs out.
  • Faster = more points! A time bonus is added for quick answers.
  • Build a streak of correct answers for a score multiplier (up to ×2).
  • Use ✂️ 50/50 once per game to remove two wrong answers.
  • Your category high score is saved automatically.
